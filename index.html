HACK BOĞAZİÇİ

You are invited to Boğaziçi DataCamp organized by Boğaziçi University IT Club Compec for the first time this year.

During the two-day summit, with talks, you will recognize the business applications of data science with a technical approach and practice them in workshops.

Get Your Ticket Free

What

Boğaziçi DataCamp is a two-day summit at which the field of data science with its business applications is discussed via talks and workshops are held where attenders can learn by practicing. It will be organized for the first time by Boğaziçi University IT Club Compec this year.

Place

Boğaziçi University
New Hall 105
BM B4 (For workshops)

Time

17-18 November

For Whom

Boğaziçi DataCamp is designed for you, if

  • You are a student from the departments like Computer Engineering, Management & Information Systems in which programing concepts and algorithmic approaches are covered,
    (if you are a professional, still welcome)
  • You desire to make use of "data science" in your projects and career path,
  • You are curious what "data science" implies with the related terms Machine Learning, Artificial Intelligence, Image Recognition, etc.

Prizes

  • First Place: 4000 TL
  • Second Place: 2000 TL
  • Third Place: Surprise Prizes!
